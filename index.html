7. Seeming Sealed Appear Closed:
Do: Use Wuji Stance to- Gather your hands to cross, palms facing you, at shoulder height.  The right hand on the outside.  Then, Step out and even with the right foot into a Horse Stance. 8. Wave Hands Like Clouds
Do: Use Wuji.  Lower left hand to below hip level, the palm facing the leg.  Right hand is at the height of the left elbow with palm facing down. Then, Step out and even with the right foot into a Horse Stance.  When you first touch your heel for the Horse Stance, the right hand traces a smile while the left hand traces a rainbow.  These tracings will look like two circles while you do the motion.  Then the hands will end in the starting position, but the hands switched their roles with the right hand lower and the left hand higher.  Step in with the left foot into Wuji.  Subtly load your weight to the right and trace the rainbow and smile again, so that you can return to the starting position with the hands in the starting orientation, ready for the wind up.  Repeat this 2x.  On the 3rd and final swing, you end in position with the right hand lower and the left hand higher.  End in Wuji stance.

13. Single Whip
Do: In this Angled Horse Stance, place the foot and load while you secure with the right hand and press downward.  The left hand rotates as you push shoulder height toward the direction of your horse stance, your push will leverage upwards but not above shoulder height. Your target is toward the shadow shoulder/elbow.  
Next Up: Double Press
Move: Step up with the right foot into a Horse Stance.

14. Double Press
Do: Press both hands downward while you aim accurately your right foot into the horse stance.  Look downwards while you press down.  
Next Up: White Crane Flashes Wings
Move: Use the Horse Stance.

15. White Crane Flashes Wings
Do:  Once your hands are lowered press up with the right hand while you press down with the left hand.  The right hand will trace a big semicircle toward the right while the left traces a big semicircle toward the left.  They end at switched positions in the center.  With the left hand on top, the left hand lowers to the center.  With the right hand on the bottom it raises to the center.  Once at the center, push with both hands forward.  Form claw-hand shape with hands once the push is done and lower the hands to navel level.   
Next Up: Closing Form
Move: Use Horse Stance.

16. Closing Form
Do: Use Horsestance. Lift both hands. Draw in. Press down.
